StarVLA는 다음 프로젝트에서 영감을 받았습니다:

- [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot)
- [GR00T](https://github.com/NVIDIA/Isaac-GR00T/tree/main)
- [DeepSpeed](https://github.com/deepspeedai/DeepSpeed)
- [Qwen-VL](https://github.com/QwenLM/Qwen3-VL/tree/main)
- [InternVL](https://github.com/OpenGVLab/InternVL)

이 코드베이스는 원래 [InternVLA-M1](https://github.com/InternRobotics/InternVLA-M1)에서 포크되었습니다.
